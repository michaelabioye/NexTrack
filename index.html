NexTrack

A coding Challenge

SuperDog Events is an event company the produces comic cons. The marketing team has requested a site that displays stats from their events. You are given an array of event data to build a site.

The objectives of NexTrack are the following:

  • Display total event attendence by city
  • Display average events attendence by city
  • Display the most and least attendend events by city
  • Display the above stats for all events in all cities
  • Add new events and store them in local storage
